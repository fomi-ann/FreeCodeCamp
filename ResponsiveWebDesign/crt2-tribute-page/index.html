World Turtle Day

World Turtle Day is an annual observance held every May 23rd. It began in 2000 and is sponsored by American Tortoise Rescue. The day was created as a yearly observance to help people celebrate and protect turtles and tortoises and their disappearing habitats, as well as to encourage human action to help them survive and thrive.

Celebrate turtles with the whole world by

  • dressing up as turtles
  • wearing green summer dresses
  • saving turtles caught on highways
  • research activities
  • adopting turtles or tortoises from rehabilitation centers

Did you know?

American Tortoise Rescue provides Turtle Day lesson plans and craft projects, to encourage teaching about turtles in classrooms.
